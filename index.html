About Me

My Journey

I'm Michael Onyemazu, a 21‑year‑old creative based in Lagos, Nigeria. Self‑taught in web development, I transformed my curiosity into skills—turning late‑night YouTube tutorials into real, responsive websites built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm passionate about creating interfaces that are not just functional, but intuitive and delightful.

Beyond code, I bring 3+ years of hands‑on experience in customer service and 1 year of marketing experience. I've learned how to listen deeply, understand user needs, and communicate solutions with empathy and clarity. This unique blend of technical skills and human-centered thinking is what drives my work—I build with both logic and heart.

Today, I focus on crafting digital experiences that solve real problems. My approach combines clean code practices, design thinking, and a genuine commitment to understanding the people who use what I build. I'm driven by curiosity, attention to detail, and a desire to create meaningful impact through thoughtful design.
