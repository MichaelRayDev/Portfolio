About Me

I'm Michael Onyemazu, a 21‑year‑old creative from Lagos, Nigeria. I started my journey teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript out of pure curiosity—turning late‑night YouTube tutorials into real, responsive websites. That same hunger for learning led me to design tools like Canva and CorelDRAW, and even dabbling in Figma.

But technology is only half of my story. My 3+ years in face‑to‑face and remote customer service taught me how to listen, de‑escalate, and communicate with empathy. I've been promoted within ten months for knowing my product inside out and treating every user interaction as a chance to build trust.

Today I combine both worlds: writing clean code while keeping the human experience at the center. I'm looking for a remote front‑end role where I can grow, contribute, and continue learning.
